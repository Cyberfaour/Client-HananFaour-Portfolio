Hanan Faour

Welcome to the World of Language Mastery!

Meet Hanan Faour, a licensed Translator, Interpreter, and an accomplished English Teacher. With a proven track record in teaching English and Arabic to a diverse group of learners, Hanan has transformed language learning into an engaging and enriching experience.

From guiding students to pass standardized exams like IELTS and EmSAT to helping underachieving students catch up post-COVID-19 lockdowns, Hanan's approach is tailored to each learner's unique needs. Her expertise in teaching both General English and Modern Standard Arabic has led to significant improvements in her students' conversational fluency and comprehension skills.

Dive into the journey of language learning with Hanan and unlock new opportunities in the global arena!
